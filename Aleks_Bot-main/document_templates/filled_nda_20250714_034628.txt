NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into on this July 14, 2025 by and between:

Director Krennic
with a principal place of business at Scarif (hereinafter referred to as "Disclosing Party");

AND

Lord Vader
with a principal place of business at Mustafar (hereinafter referred to as "Receiving Party").

WHEREAS, the Disclosing Party possesses certain confidential and proprietary information relating to Death Star;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. Definition of Confidential Information. "Confidential Information" shall mean any and all information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party, including without limitation Blueprints, Construction Logistics.

2. Obligations of Receiving Party. The Receiving Party agrees to hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and not to disclose it to any third party.

3. Term. This Agreement shall be effective as of the date first written above and shall continue for a period of 24 months.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
